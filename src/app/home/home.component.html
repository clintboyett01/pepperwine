RV Park Near Jacksonville, TX - Great Location and Amenities


Pepperwine is a beautiful 54 acre RV park located off highway 69 outside of Jacksonville Texas. We offer 4 scenic hiking trails, an impressive 5 acre catch-and-release fishing lake, and easy access to highway 69. Our amenities include nine 75x12’ concrete RV pads, four 60x12' concrete RV pads, 10x10’ concrete patio with picnic tables, full 30/50 amp hookups, sewage, and trash pickup. Pepperwine RV Park is proud to offer all this to ensure our customers have the perfect RV experience."
